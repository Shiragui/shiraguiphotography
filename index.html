Who I Am

Hi, I’m Shira Gui, an undergraduate at Columbia University studying Computer Science (SEAS '28). When I’m not coding or working on projects, you’ll find me behind the lens of my Canon EOS R6, capturing the world around me. Photography has been a passion of mine for years—whether I’m traveling, exploring the city, looking at cute animals, or taking pictures for friends, I believe in preserving moments that matter.
