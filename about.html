About Me

Hi, I'm Shira Gui!

I’m a Columbia University undergrad studying Computer Science, but photography is where I get to slow down and really connect—with people, places, and stories. I’m based in both Wisconsin and New York City.

Over time, I moved from a hand-me-down camera to my Canon EOS R6. I specialize in portraits, graduation sessions, and headshots—but more than that, I care about making people feel comfortable and confident in front of the camera.

If you're looking for a photographer who cares about the little moments and is as invested in your photos as you are, I’d love to work with you.
